Recent Work: Spot Vision LLC

Product Marketing Manager & Prototype Developer

Spot Vision is a machine learning lab revolutionizing scannable design and encoded graphics. Our proprietary technology embeds dynamic data directly into visual designs, transforming graphics into interactive digital touchpoints. We focus on creating high-tech, scannable designs for fashion-tech, collectibles, product packaging, and interactive media.

As Product Marketing Manager, I prototyped use cases such as collectible art pieces, interactive apparel, scannable stickers, and product packaging tape — merging physical products with digital experiences.

I led content production, managed our social media presence, and created commercial marketing assets. This included overseeing photo shoots, sourcing models and photographers, and developing visual prototypes to market our technology and future applications.

To strengthen industry connections, I organized community meetups and industry gatherings, positioning Spot Vision at the intersection of design and machine learning innovation.
