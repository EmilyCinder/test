Rift Apparel: Solving Problems with Encoded Fashion

Rift Apparel tackles key challenges in fashion by embedding encoded, scannable graphics into garments. This transforms clothing into a digital bridge — combating counterfeiting, enabling interactivity, and enriching brand storytelling.

The Problem

The fashion industry lacks scalable ways to merge authentication, collectibility, and interactivity. Counterfeiting and impersonal brand interactions remain widespread. The challenge: can we design clothing that is secure, digital-integrated, and expressive?

The Solution

Rift is a prototype clothing line that embeds encoded graphics into wearable designs. Each item is scannable and linked to digital data, turning fashion into a collectible and interactive product. The project explored the entire pipeline: sourcing, pattern design, digital linkage, branding, and production.

Problem-Solving Approach

  • Identified issues in fashion authentication and brand engagement.
  • Created fabric-safe encoded designs that could stretch and print cleanly.
  • Built a brand identity — Rift — representing tech disruption and digital evolution.
  • Developed scannable digital assets using Illustrator and Spot Vision’s platform.
  • Tested user interactions and documented performance insights.

Prototype Development

Blanks were sourced to determine compatibility with encoded printing. The design team adjusted patterns for hoods, sleeves, and other segments, ensuring clarity in scanning. Rift branding and digital tags were embedded into each piece, tracked by a secure system.

Mockups, photoshoots, and internal documentation supported scalability analysis and marketing strategy for potential launch phases.

Outcomes

The Rift Apparel prototype proved that encoded, interactive fashion is possible using accessible tools and strong branding. This system opens the door for future collections that are both functional and secure — turning fashion into an experience.
